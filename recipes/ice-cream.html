Creamy Ice Cream!

This is the smoothest frozen treat you will ever experience.

The sensation is so cold it feels like a warm.

    Ingredients
  • Water 1
  • Water 2
  • Water 3
  • White Food Color
  • just a little dirt
    Steps
  1. Mix Waters 1 and 3 until bubbly
  2. Pour that into Water 2
  3. Lid and shake container containing all waters until it sloshes a little too much
  4. squirt white into water to create the vanilla flavoring
  5. Add pinches of soil or dirt to include the vanilla bean
  6. Enjoy!?!
