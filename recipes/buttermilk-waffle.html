Buttermilk Waffles

Very Angry Milk Waffle Recipe

These waffles will crackle in the morning sun.

Best topped with strawberries and more milk.

    Ingredients
  • Milk 1
  • Milk 2
  • Milk 3
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Lemon Juice
    Steps
  1. Mix Milks 1 and 3 until frothy
  2. Pour that into Milk 2
  3. Lid and shake container containing all milk until it yelps
  4. Now squeeze lemon juice into milks until yelping turns to curious 'hmm's
  5. Heat pan to either 5 or 8 depending on how inquisitive the 'hmm's appear
  6. Press inquring milk onto pan and wait until it seems satisfied
  7. Take off heat and enjoy!
