Very Dairy Lasanga Recipe

This lasagna is very milky. It is make with the milk of at least 3 cows and each cow should reside from a different climate.

The term used by locals to rate it's milk content is "ooky-gooky-ness'.

    Ingredients
  • Milk 1
  • Milk 2
  • Milk 3
  • Cheese
  • A big tomato
  • Wheat
    Steps
  1. Mix Milks 1 and 3 until frothy
  2. Pour that into Milk 2
  3. Lid and shake container containing all milk until it yelps
  4. Roll the big tomato (one) in a pile of wheat
  5. Dunk tomato into yelping milks and enjoy!
